Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our wellness application ("সেমিয়ন হেলথ"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We may collect basic information such as name, email, or demographic data if you choose to provide it.
  • Wellness Data: This includes sleep patterns, heart rate, activity levels, and other wellness metrics.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and usage patterns may be collected to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve app features and user experience.
  • To analyze trends and generate anonymized wellness insights.
  • To respond to user support requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We take steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share anonymized data with researchers or analytics services to improve public health outcomes or our app performance.

5. User Control

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at contact@semion.ai. You may also disable data collection features in the app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services (e.g., cloud storage, analytics). These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@semion.ai.
